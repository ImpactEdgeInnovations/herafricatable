# Landing Page Content Requirements

The landing-page structure and launch copy are implemented. These authentic inputs
are required before the public launch; they must not be replaced with invented social
proof.

## Brand

- Final logo files: SVG preferred, plus square mark and light/dark variants
- Confirmed brand tagline, colors and typeface licenses
- Final custom domain and working support/privacy email addresses
- Social profiles that should appear in the footer

## First Nairobi event

- Event name, date, start/end times and timezone
- Venue name or the approved wording if the venue remains private
- Ticket types, capacity, price/currency and sales-opening date
- Final event description and dress/accessibility information
- Programme highlights, hosts and confirmed speakers
- Payment/refund/cancellation terms

## Authentic media

- 8–12 approved high-resolution event or community photographs
- One landscape hero image/video option
- Speaker/host headshots with names, roles and usage permission
- Sponsor/partner logos with approval and preferred linking
- Captions, alt text context and photographer credits

## Social proof

- Three to six member testimonials in the member's own words
- Member name, role/company and explicit publication consent
- Any verified participation, impact or engagement figures
- Press or partner quotes with permission and source links

## Legal and operations

- Legal controller/business entity and privacy contact
- Final Privacy Notice, Terms, Community Guidelines and cookie position
- Primary launch jurisdictions and approved retention/refund policies
- Support hours and escalation contact

## Publishing rule

Until approved content is supplied, the page uses editorial product illustrations and
clearly labels event details as forthcoming. Do not publish placeholder testimonials,
fake attendance numbers, unconfirmed logos, or generated images as documentary proof.
